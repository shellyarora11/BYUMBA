Path to Global Domination

Shelly Arora

Future Career Path

I will be joining Amazon as a Pathways Operations Manager in Fort Worth, Texas this summer. The pathways program allows me to become a general manager within 4 years. I will be able to make a greater influence in the world through the resources Amazon will provide me.

Field Experience

I worked with Amazon last summer and absolutely loved my exprerience. I was able to save $2.2 M within weeks of employment. My extensive experience in operations really helped me be successful at Amazon. My regional director taught me that when we are recommending a solution, we must always keep in mind that our recommendations should make a global impact. Does our solution help our associates in Sri Lanka or Brazil? If not, then we must go back to our team and figure out a better solution. Learn and Be curious and Think Big.

Anticipated Contributions

My long term goal is to set up a scholarship fund with BYU, BYU Hawaii, BYU Idaho and Ensign College for students that are under-privileged or are orphans who aspire to go to college. I also have a deep desire to open boarding schools for poor and orphan children in India and Africa. I want to sponsor their college education and give them the opportunity to live their dreams. Many children lost parents and guardians in the pandemic and natural disasters. This is the least I can do to give back to the society.
